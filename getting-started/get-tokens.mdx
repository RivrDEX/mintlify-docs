title: "Get tokens"
description: "Acquire VARA and other tokens on Vara Network so you can start trading on RivrDEX."
---

To trade or provide liquidity on RivrDEX, you need tokens in your wallet on Vara Network. How you get them depends on whether you're using testnet or mainnet.

<Note>RivrDEX currently operates on testnet — use TVARA for all transactions.</Note>

## Testnet: get TVARA from a faucet

TVARA (test VARA) has no real-world value and is used solely for testing on the Vara testnet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Gear IDEA faucet">
    Go to [idea.gear-tech.io/programs](https://idea.gear-tech.io/programs). This is the Gear IDEA platform, which includes a built-in faucet for the Vara testnet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Connect your Vara-compatible wallet to the Gear IDEA platform using the same steps you used for RivrDEX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request TVARA">
    Use the faucet to request TVARA. Tokens are sent directly to your connected wallet address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your balance">
    Open your wallet — your TVARA balance should appear within a few seconds. You're now ready to swap on RivrDEX.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For more information about Vara Network and its testnet, visit [vara.network](https://vara.network).

<Tip>Start with a small amount to get familiar with the interface before making larger transactions.</Tip>

## Mainnet: bridge assets to Vara Network

On mainnet, you can move assets from other networks to Vara using a bridge.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the Gear bridge">
    Go to [gear-bridges.app](https://gear-bridges.app) to bridge tokens from supported networks to Vara Network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your asset and amount">
    Choose the asset you want to bridge, enter the amount, and confirm the destination address on Vara.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the transaction">
    Approve the transaction in your wallet. Bridging times vary depending on the source network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your wallet">
    Once the bridge completes, your tokens will appear in your Vara wallet. You can then connect to RivrDEX and start swapping.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next

Once you have tokens in your wallet, head to the [Swap page](/features/swapping) to make your first trade, or visit [Pools](/features/providing-liquidity) to earn fees by providing liquidity.
