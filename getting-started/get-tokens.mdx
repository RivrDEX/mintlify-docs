To trade or provide liquidity on RivrDEX, you need tokens in your Vara wallet. The process depends on whether you're on testnet or mainnet.

<Note>
  RivrDEX currently operates on testnet. Use TVARA and wrapped testnet tokens for all transactions.
</Note>

## Testnet: bridge assets using Vara Network Bridge

The Vara Network Bridge allows you to move tokens between Ethereum (Holesky testnet) and Vara testnet. Supported assets: TVARA, WTVARA, WUSDC, WBTC, WETH, WUSDT.

<Warning>
  Make sure you switch to **Testnet** mode in the bridge before proceeding. The network selector is in the top-left corner of the bridge interface — set it to **Testnet (Vara Testnet · Ethereum Hoodi)**, not Mainnet.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the bridge">
    Go to [bridge.vara.network](https://bridge.vara.network/?network=testnet). The link opens directly in Testnet mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallets">
    Connect both your **MetaMask** (for Ethereum side) and your **Vara-compatible wallet** such as SubWallet or Polkadot.js extension (for Vara side). Both wallet addresses will appear in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get testnet tokens from the faucet">
    Go to the **My Tokens & Faucet** tab. You'll see your balances on both Vara and Ethereum. Use the **Get Balance** button next to USDC, WBTC, USDT, or ETH to claim free testnet tokens directly to your Ethereum wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bridge tokens to Vara">
    Go back to the **Bridge** tab. Select the token you want to send, enter the amount, and paste your Vara wallet address in the **Bridge to** field — or click **Use Wallet Address** to fill it automatically. Leave Claim Type on **Automatic** for the simplest experience. Review the expected fee (~0.000125 ETH) and bridge time (~20 min), then click **Submit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for confirmation">
    Bridging takes approximately **20 minutes**. You can track the status in the **Transactions** tab. Once complete, your wrapped tokens (e.g. WETH, WUSDT) will appear in your Vara wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If your transaction appears stuck after the first signature, don't worry — your tokens are locked but safe. The bridge UI will notify you to sign a second pending transaction to complete the transfer.
</Tip>

## Mainnet: bridge assets to Vara Network

On mainnet, the same bridge supports transfers between Ethereum mainnet and Vara mainnet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the bridge on Mainnet">
    Go to [bridge.vara.network](https://bridge.vara.network) and make sure the network selector in the top-left shows **Mainnet (Vara Mainnet · Ethereum Mainnet)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallets">
    Connect MetaMask for the Ethereum side and your Vara-compatible wallet for the Vara side.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select asset and amount">
    Choose the token you want to bridge (VARA, ETH, BTC, USDT, or USDC), enter the amount, and confirm the destination address on Vara.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and wait">
    Approve the transaction in your wallet. Bridging takes approximately 20 minutes depending on network conditions. Track progress in the **Transactions** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported tokens

| Token | Ethereum side | Vara side |
|---|---|---|

| Ethereum | ETH | wETH |

| Tether | USDT | wUSDT |
| USD Coin | USDC | wUSDC |
