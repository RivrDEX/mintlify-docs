RivrDEX is live on **Vara Network mainnet**. This is the fastest path from zero to your first real swap. Each step links to a detailed guide if you want more depth.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up a Vara wallet">
    Install [SubWallet](https://subwallet.app) (recommended) or another Vara-compatible extension, and create or import an account.

    Full guide: [Connect a wallet](/getting-started/connect-wallet)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get VARA and tokens">
    Bridge assets from Ethereum with the [Vara Network Bridge](https://bridge.vara.network) — VARA, ETH, BTC, USDT, and USDC are supported. Bridging takes about 20 minutes.

    Full guide: [Get tokens](/getting-started/get-tokens)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the app and connect">
    Go to [app.rivrdex.io](https://app.rivrdex.io), click **Connect Wallet** in the top-right corner, and approve the connection in your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first swap">
    Pick a pair on the **Swap** page, enter an amount, review the rate and price impact, and confirm. You pay a flat **0.35% fee** and no gas on top.

    Full guide: [Swapping tokens](/features/swapping)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start earning (optional)">
    Two ways to earn on RivrDEX from day one:

    - **[Rewards](/rewards/overview)** — points for every swap, redeemable for USDT, hardware wallets, and roles. Verify your wallet at [rivrdex.io/rewards](https://rivrdex.io/rewards) *before* trading so every swap counts.
    - **[Provide liquidity](/features/providing-liquidity)** — deposit token pairs into pools and earn the 0.3% LP share of the fee on every trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Joining Rewards first takes two minutes and costs nothing — trading points and missions only count activity made **after** your wallet is verified.
</Tip>
