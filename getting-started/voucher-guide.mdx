title: "First swap with the voucher"
description: "A visual, step-by-step walkthrough: bridge USDT, then make your first RivrDEX swap with zero VARA using the gas voucher."
---

The voucher lets you make your **first swap without holding any VARA** to pay the
network fee — and that first swap is exactly how you get the VARA that covers
every swap afterward. Here's the full flow, screen by screen.

<Note>
  Prefer a text reference over screenshots? [Get tokens](/getting-started/get-tokens)
  covers the same flow in a shorter, no-images format.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Vara Bridge and connect both wallets">
    Go to the [Vara Bridge](https://bridge.vara.network/?network=mainnet) and
    connect your **EVM wallet** (Ethereum side) and your **Vara wallet** (Vara side).

    You'll bridge **USDT** from Ethereum. If you don't have USDT yet, you can get
    some quickly through the [RivrDEX cross-chain swap](https://app.rivrdex.io/cross-chain)
    (powered by Delora).

    <Frame caption="The Vara Bridge — USDT (Ethereum) to WUSDT (Vara), with both wallets connected">
      <img src="/images/voucher/step-1-bridge-connect.png" alt="Vara Bridge interface showing USDT to WUSDT and Transfer Settings" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the USDT → WUSDT pair">
    Set the pair to **USDT (Ethereum Mainnet) → WUSDT (Vara Mainnet)** and enter
    the amount. Paste your Vara address in the **Bridge to** field, or click
    **Use Wallet Address** to fill it automatically.

    <Warning>
      **Bridge USDT only.** RivrDEX currently trades against **wUSDT** — bridging
      ETH or USDC gives you wrapped tokens you can't swap yet.
    </Warning>

    <Frame caption="Choose USDT → WUSDT and set the destination Vara address">
      <img src="/images/voucher/step-2-select-pair.png" alt="Bridge pair selection: USDT to WUSDT with Bridge-to address field" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the claim type">
    In **Transfer Settings**, pick how your tokens arrive:

    - **Automatic** — tokens land in your wallet on their own, but you pay a small
      extra fee in **ETH**.
    - **Manual** — no extra ETH fee, but you claim the tokens yourself afterward,
      which costs a fee in **VARA**.

    <Frame caption="Automatic vs Manual claim in Transfer Settings">
      <img src="/images/voucher/step-3-claim-type.png" alt="Claim type selector showing Automatic and Manual options" />
    </Frame>

    <Warning>
      If this is your first bridge and you have **no VARA yet**, choose **Automatic**.
      Manual claiming needs VARA for gas — exactly what you don't have yet.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm and wait for the bridge (~20 min)">
    Confirm the transaction in your wallet and wait — bridging takes about
    **20 minutes**.

    If you chose **Manual**, open the
    [Transactions page](https://bridge.vara.network/transactions?network=mainnet)
    after the bridge finishes and claim your tokens there.

    <Frame caption="Manual claims are completed on the Transactions page">
      <img src="/images/voucher/step-4-manual-claim.png" alt="Vara Bridge Transactions page for manual claiming" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open RivrDEX and connect your wallet">
    Head to [RivrDEX](https://app.rivrdex.io/trade) and connect your Vara wallet.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Swap WUSDT → VARA">
    Select the **WUSDT / VARA** pair, choose the amount, and confirm the swap.

    <Frame caption="Swap WUSDT → VARA on RivrDEX">
      <img src="/images/voucher/step-6-swap.png" alt="RivrDEX swap interface for WUSDT to VARA" />
    </Frame>

    <Tip>
      **Your first swap is free.** RivrDEX covers the network fee for it — it works
      even with a zero VARA balance. After it settles you're holding VARA, which
      pays the gas for everything you do next.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Convert WVARA → VARA">
    Some swaps deliver **WVARA** (wrapped VARA). It's the same value — just convert
    it to native VARA in one click when the **Receive VARA** dialog appears.

    <Frame caption="Convert WVARA to native VARA right after the swap">
      <img src="/images/voucher/step-7-convert.png" alt="Convert WVARA to VARA dialog" />
    </Frame>

    Missed the dialog? Open the bridge's **My Tokens** tab and convert it there.

    <Frame caption="You can also convert from the My Tokens tab on the Vara Bridge">
      <img src="/images/voucher/step-7-my-tokens.png" alt="My Tokens tab on the Vara Bridge showing a WVARA balance to convert" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's it — you used the voucher for your first swap, and you now hold enough VARA
to cover the fees on every swap that follows.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need VARA in my wallet to use the voucher for my first swap?">
    No. You don't need any VARA to pay gas on your first swap — the network fee is
    covered for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the claim type after I've confirmed the bridge?">
    No. The claim type (Automatic or Manual) is fixed once you confirm.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which exchanges can I buy VARA on?">
    [Coinbase](https://www.coinbase.com/price/vara-network),
    [Gate.io](https://www.gate.io/trade/VARA_USDT), and
    [Crypto.com](https://crypto.com/price/vara-network). Withdraw on the native
    Vara Network — see [Get tokens](/getting-started/get-tokens) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use the voucher on my second swap?">
    No. The voucher only covers your **first** swap. After that you pay the standard
    fee, using the VARA you received from that first swap.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
