Everything you earn also builds **XP**. XP is your lifetime total of earned points — spending points in the shop never lowers it. As XP grows you climb levels, and levels pay you back twice: a **loyalty multiplier** on all future trading points, and access to better shop rewards.

## The ten levels

| Level | XP required | Loyalty bonus | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | — | Roles, whitelist |
| 2 | 250 | — | $5 USDT reward |
| 3 | 600 | +5% | $10 USDT, gift cards |
| 4 | 1,100 | +5% | $25 USDT reward |
| 5 | 1,700 | +10% | SafePal, exclusive Limited |
| 6 | 2,500 | +10% | Higher daily standing |
| 7 | 3,600 | +15% | Ledger hardware wallet |
| 8 | 5,200 | +15% | Elite Limited drops |
| 9 | 7,200 | +20% | Priority fulfilment |
| 10 | 10,000 | +25% | Grand prize, leaderboard weight |

## How the loyalty multiplier works

The multiplier applies to **trading points** as they are credited. At Level 5, a 100-point trading day is credited as 110. Check-ins, missions, and role bonuses are fixed amounts and are not multiplied.

Your current multiplier is always visible on the dashboard's loyalty tile, along with what it takes to reach the next one.

<Tip>
  The multiplier is applied when points are credited, not retroactively — leveling up today makes every future trading day pay more.
</Tip>

## Leaderboard

The **Leaderboard** tab at [rivrdex.io/rewards](https://rivrdex.io/rewards) ranks the top 50 wallets by lifetime XP. Wallets are shown as shortened addresses — the same open, block-explorer style as the rest of the program.

If you're outside the top 50, your own row still appears with your exact rank, so you always know where you stand.
