RivrDEX Rewards is the loyalty program for traders on Vara Network. Every swap you make earns points. Points build your XP and level, unlock loyalty bonuses, and can be spent in the rewards shop — on USDT payouts, hardware wallets, and exclusive Discord roles.

The rewards hub lives at [rivrdex.io/rewards](https://rivrdex.io/rewards).

<Note>
  Your wallet is your rewards identity. There is no sign-up form — connect a SubWallet, prove you own it with a free signature, and you're in. No funds ever leave your wallet.
</Note>

## Your rewards identity

- **Wallet-first.** Your Vara wallet (SubWallet) is the account. All trading activity is read directly from the chain — there is nothing to report manually.
- **One free signature.** To join, you sign a short message that proves you own the wallet. It costs nothing and moves no funds.
- **Discord is optional** — until you want to claim role bonuses or redeem from the shop. One wallet links to one Discord account, permanently, to keep the campaign fair.
- **Transparent by design.** Balances and history are keyed to wallet addresses, the same way a block explorer works.

<Warning>
  Only activity **after** you verify your wallet counts toward missions and trading points. Verify first, then trade.
</Warning>

## When points arrive

Trading points for a given day (UTC) are credited shortly after **00:00 UTC**, once the day closes. While the day is still running, the dashboard shows a live **"Earning today"** counter with your pending total, updated as you trade.

Check-ins, mission rewards, and role bonuses are credited instantly.
