The shop is where points turn into rewards. Every item is **one-time per account** — once you've redeemed something, it's yours, and the slot is used. This keeps rewards spread across the community instead of being farmed by a few accounts.

**Trading days** are days on which you made at least one swap — they show as "active days" on your dashboard. **Min volume** is your cumulative swap volume since you verified your wallet. Together the gates reward genuine, consistent trading rather than a single big day or dust-sized swaps.

<Note>
  Availability can change during the season — some items are limited stock. The shop page always shows the current state.
</Note>

## How to redeem

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the shop">
    Go to the **Shop** tab at [rivrdex.io/rewards](https://rivrdex.io/rewards). Items you can afford and have unlocked show an active **Redeem** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Discord">
    Redeeming requires a linked Discord account. If you haven't linked one, the button offers to connect it first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the checklist in the redeem dialog — points balance, level, trading days, Discord — and confirm. Points are deducted immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your reward">
    - **Exclusive Role** — granted to your Discord account automatically, usually within seconds.
    - **USDT coupons** — paid out to your verified wallet; you can provide an EVM address for the payout in the dialog.
    - **Hardware wallets** — fulfilled manually by the team; you'll be contacted through Discord to arrange shipping.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fair-play rules

- **One wallet, one Discord.** The first redemption permanently pairs your wallet with your Discord account. Neither can be reused with another account — this is the campaign's anti-abuse anchor.
- **One redemption per item.** Every reward is once per account, enforced on-chain-style in the points ledger.
- **Server-side checks.** Level, trading days, and Discord requirements are verified on the backend at redeem time — not just in the interface.
