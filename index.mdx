## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Install a Vara-compatible wallet extension and connect it to the RivrDEX app at [stg-app.rivrdex.io](https://stg-app.rivrdex.io).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get tokens on Vara">
    Bridge assets to Vara Network or claim tokens from a faucet if you're on testnet. See [Get Tokens](/docs/getting-started/get-tokens) for options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading or earning">
    Swap tokens instantly, add liquidity to earn fees, or create a new trading pair — all permissionlessly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
