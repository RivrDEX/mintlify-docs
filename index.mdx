RivrDEX is a permissionless, non-custodial decentralized exchange live on **Vara Network mainnet**. Trade any token, earn from liquidity pools, collect rewards for every swap, or launch your own trading pair — with near-zero gas costs and instant execution.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Install a Vara-compatible wallet extension and connect it to the RivrDEX app at [app.rivrdex.io](https://app.rivrdex.io). See [Connect a wallet](/getting-started/connect-wallet).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get tokens on Vara">
    Bridge VARA, ETH, BTC, USDT, or USDC to Vara mainnet with the Vara Network Bridge. See [Get tokens](/getting-started/get-tokens) for the walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading or earning">
    Swap tokens instantly, add liquidity to earn fees, or join [Rewards](/rewards/overview) and earn points on every trade.
  </Step>
</Steps>
