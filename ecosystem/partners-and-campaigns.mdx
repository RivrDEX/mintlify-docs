RivrDEX is not just a DEX — it is the DeFi infrastructure layer for the Vara ecosystem. As more projects launch on Vara, RivrDEX provides the liquidity and trading primitives they need to function.

## Galxe quests and campaigns

RivrDEX runs community campaigns on [Galxe](https://galxe.com) to reward early adopters and recognize active community members.

Completing quests on Galxe can earn you:

- On-chain credentials and badges
- Ecosystem recognition and early-adopter status
- Rewards tied to specific campaigns as they are announced

To find active RivrDEX campaigns, go to [galxe.com](https://galxe.com) and search for **"RivrDEX"**.

<Tip>
  Follow RivrDEX on X and join the Discord to stay updated on new campaigns and partner announcements.
</Tip>

## Ecosystem partnerships

RivrDEX actively partners with projects building on Vara — from token launches and protocol integrations to co-marketing and liquidity bootstrapping.

If you are building on Vara and want to explore what a partnership could look like, reach out:

- **Email**: [team@rivrdex.io](mailto:team@rivrdex.io)
- **Website**: [rivrdex.io](https://rivrdex.io)

Partnerships can include:

- **Token listings** — get your token trading on RivrDEX from day one.
- **Liquidity programs** — coordinate incentivized liquidity pools to bootstrap depth for your token.
- **Co-marketing** — joint campaigns, Galxe quests, and announcements to the RivrDEX community.
- **Protocol integrations** — integrate RivrDEX swap or liquidity functionality into your product.

<Info>
  As the Vara ecosystem grows, more partner integrations and campaigns will be announced through official RivrDEX channels on X and Discord.
</Info>
